Discover Udaipur

Udaipur, also known as the "Venice of the East", is a historic city situated in Rajasthan, India. With its stunning lakes, beautiful palaces, and vibrant culture, Udaipur is a must-visit destination for any traveler. The city is famous for the Lake Palace, City Palace, and Jag Mandir, among many other landmarks. Whether you're here to explore the rich history, relax by the lakeside, or enjoy traditional Rajasthani cuisine, Udaipur has something for everyone. Learn more about the city here.
